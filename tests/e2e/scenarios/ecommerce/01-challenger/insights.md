# Challenger Phase Insights — E-commerce Platform

## Reasoning Log

### Problem Space Exploration
- Initial hypothesis: SMBs need cheaper e-commerce solutions
- Refined understanding: Cost is secondary to simplicity and time-to-value
- Evidence: Market research shows 60% of SMB e-commerce projects abandoned due to complexity

### Root Cause Methodology
- Applied 5 Whys analysis to surface underlying issues
- Cross-referenced with competitor analysis (Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce)
- Identified gap: No solution optimizes for "zero-to-sales-in-2-hours"

## Trade-offs Considered

| Decision | Options | Chosen | Rationale |
|----------|---------|--------|-----------|
| Scope | Full platform vs. MVP | MVP | Validate core hypothesis first |
| Target market | All SMBs vs. specific vertical | General SMB | Broader validation opportunity |

## Discarded Alternatives

1. **Mobile app only** - Rejected due to web-first customer behavior in target segment
2. **Marketplace model** - Out of scope, changes business model fundamentally
3. **White-label existing solution** - Doesn't address root cause of complexity

- Are there regulatory nuances by geography to prioritize?
